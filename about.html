About Adore Hair & Nail Studio

Adore Hair and Nail Studio strives to be one step ahead of the trends and is confident you will walk out feeling the best version of yourself on the inside and out.


Adore Hair and Nail Studio was established in December of 2013 and is located at 6099 Gratiot in Saginaw!


The upscale atmosphere is sure to draw you in and the stylist and staff is very warm welcoming and friendly. There is a reason Adore was voted "best of the best" in 2017 after only 3 years of opening its doors! Come find out for yourself what everyone is talking about!
